# Workforce & Employee Analytics Intelligence System

## Project Overview

The Workforce & Employee Analytics Intelligence System is an end-to-end HR analytics solution developed to help organizations understand employee behavior, identify attrition patterns, and support workforce decision-making through analytics and predictive modeling.

The project combines business analytics, workforce analytics, and machine learning to provide actionable insights for HR teams and management.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations often struggle to identify the reasons behind employee attrition, low employee satisfaction, and workforce inefficiencies. High attrition can lead to increased hiring costs, productivity loss, and reduced employee engagement.

This project aims to analyze workforce data, identify key attrition drivers, and predict employees who may be at risk of leaving the organization.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze employee attrition trends.
* Understand factors affecting employee retention.
* Evaluate employee satisfaction and work-life balance.
* Identify workforce patterns across departments and job roles.
* Predict employee attrition risk using machine learning.
* Generate HR recommendations for employee retention.

## Key Findings

* Employees working overtime showed higher attrition rates.
* Lower job satisfaction contributed to employee turnover.
* Work-life balance influenced employee retention.
* Certain departments experienced higher attrition.
* Employee risk prediction helped identify potential attrition cases.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Reduce excessive overtime.
* Improve employee engagement initiatives.
* Conduct regular satisfaction surveys.
* Monitor high-risk employees.
* Improve work-life balance programs.
* Develop retention strategies for at-risk employees.
